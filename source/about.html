About me

I'm a Ukrainian junior web-developer currently based in Wroclaw, Poland.

Looking for new challenges and experiences, in June 2018 I started my journey into web development. I’ve learned really a lot so far, and the credit for that goes in large part to HTML Academy. Now I’m splitting my time between coding websites and content creation.
